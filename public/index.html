Who I Am

New Jersey born and Chicago raised. I love front-end web development, music, travel, and soccer. With a background in technical theatre, and a degree in neuroscience, I wish to use my knowledge in user interface design and usability analysis. My love for music has also driven me to some studio engineering work, a passion that I have felt since my theatre days. As a recent graduate of Michigan State University, I am looking for employment that helps me feel my passion that I have felt for the other areas of work in my life.

What I Do

Web Development

Solid experience in front end web development. Extensive use of HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, and the Bootstrap framework.

Audio Engineering

Expert knowledge on both studio and live sound engineering, with mastery in ProTools, Melodyne, Qlab 3, Reaper, and Audacity. Also proficient with both analog and digital boards, such as the Yamaha LS9 and Allen & Heath GLD.

Music Composition

Years of music experience, both in composition and performance. In depth knowledge of both Finale and Sibelius, and experience transcribing sheet music from paper to digital versions.
